navigation device

A psychogeographical navigation device powered by statistical randomness and abstract user input. Based on 4 input settings -- emotion, urgency, difficulty, and either poetic, wanderer, or adventurer style -- the device guides you to random places, objects, has you meditate, and more.

trying out my psychogeographic navigation device in peckham rye park – it guided me to the only daisy in the whole field, to a lost dog collar, to an interesting collection of sticks, and made me slowly watch the sunset for 4 minutes and 55 seconds.
