Rye Lane: a 3D archival exploration

In this project, I explored both the past and present of Rye Lane Peckham using archival Google Street View. With this data, I created a 3D reconstruction of the high-street with animated textures to reveal the history of current day shops.

Compiling shopfronts

My process began with exploring and photographing locations along the highstreet, taking screenshots of the same location from various years.
