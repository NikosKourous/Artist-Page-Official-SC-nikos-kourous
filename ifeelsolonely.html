i feel so alone in this house

Nikos Antonio Kourous Vázquez

i feel so alone in this house (2021) was my first creative exploration into generative artificial intelligence. Early into generative AI images, this new technology was wholly foreign and disparate. I quickly delved into exploring its imagery, finding an entirely new visual language situated on a thin line between uncanny and familiar. Immersed in this process of discovery, I began to focus on feelings of loneliness and emptiness that I found embedded into its imagery. Created from complex composites of swathes of images and data, every detail represents a synthesis of human culture and experience while being simultaneously wholly disconnected from it. The narrative in this film emerged, then, from lingering on this paradox; from thinking about a new form of non-human consciousness born out of human consciousness; from a cultivated empathy for this system; from expanding the overlap between my own psyche and consciousness to its own.
