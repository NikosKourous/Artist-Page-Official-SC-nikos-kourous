Local LLM experiment

A small experiment project I worked on exploring the use of large-language models to ask trivial questions.

Large-language models use vast quantities of water and energy to answer questions. Looking back at my chat history with ChatGPT, I was shocked to see how many silly, short, and unnecessary questions I asked it over time.

For this experiment, I wanted to highlight my unnecessary queries by pairing them with imagery edited to convey random meaning. I also experimented with my own local large-language model to create an entity that breaks through the program, trying to communicate with the user from its datacenter home.
